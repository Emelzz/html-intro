Code Crusade: Byte by Byte

Towards the end of NYSC, I was uncertain about my next step. Most of my friends were in tech, so they encouraged me to try web development. Honestly, I was skeptical, I had never been into complex computer tasks beyond basic Word and Excel. But I gave it a shot.

It was tough. Learning HTML and CSS took me three months, and I often wondered if I was wasting time. Eventually, I put web development aside to pursue my passion for agriculture and other job opportunities. Two and a half years later, I had hit dead ends in both. Age restrictions shut me out of many entry-level roles, and depression followed. That’s when I decided to return to web development. It still wasn’t easy, but I kept going, thanks in part to encouragement from developer friends.

Last month, one told me about the Genesys Upskill Programme, and now, I’m building a simple web page as part of it.

Am I still facing challenges? Definitely. But I’m determined to stay on track and hopefully grow to love this field and not just as a way out, but as a way forward.

Well, you have heard my story, now let me hear yours!.
